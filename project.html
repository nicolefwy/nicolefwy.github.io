Bettermi.io Brand Guidelines

I developed the brand guidelines for Bettermi.io to ensure consistency between designers and marketers. These guidelines emphasize the company's mission to enhance wellness through a Web3 lifestyle dApp. I included key elements such as logo usage, color palettes, and design principles that reflect the brand’s commitment to health, fitness, and self-improvement, creating a cohesive identity across all platforms.

What is Bettermi.io?
Bettermi.io is an innovative lifestyle web3 dAPP designed to incentivize fitness and promote healthy habits. By integrating BMI fitness tracking, blockchain technology, NFTs, AI coaching, Discord community and gamification elements, Bettermi.io empowers users to take charge of their health while earning rewards. The dApp encourages individuals to maintain a healthy lifestyle by offering NFTs, exclusive perks, and crypto as incentives for their fitness achievements.

Mimi .io NFT

The team and I developed the concept for the Bettermi.io NFTs, focusing on a unique and engaging design that captures the platform's fitness-oriented mission. I designed 6000 pieces of NFT. Each NFT card features an animated character representing the user, along with three key attributes: Charm, Wisdom, and Strength.

These attributes directly impact the user's SIGDAO token rewards earned through the platform's challenges and activities. The higher the combined value of these three attributes, the greater the percentage boost to the user's SIGDAO earnings. The maximum boost potential is 5%, achieved when each attribute is at its maximum value of 5. The NFTs have three distinct levels - Level 1, Level 2, and the exclusive "Mystery Collection" Level 3 NFTs - with varying SIGDAO token earnings boosts.
